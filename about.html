Our Story

Welcome to the world of geoinformatics at Politecnico di Milano! As a leading institution in the field, we combine the power of geospatial information and cutting-edge technology to tackle pressing challenges such as landslides. Through our expertise in geoinformatics, we aim to understand, analyze, and mitigate the risks associated with landslides. Our multidisciplinary approach integrates advanced mapping techniques, remote sensing, and spatial analysis to provide valuable insights into landslide dynamics. Join us in exploring the forefront of geoinformatics research and leveraging its potential to make a positive impact on landslide management and the safety of our environment.

  • Landslide Susceptibility Assessment
  • Hazard Mapping and Zoning
  • Monitoring and Early Warning Systems
  • Landslide Inventory and Database Management
  • Engineering Solutions and Mitigation Strategies
  • Education and Awareness Programs
